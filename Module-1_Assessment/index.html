About Me

Hello world, my name is Tye. I'm looking to gain as much tech knowledge as I can through this experience. I've made a few webapps using node and react, but still have a long way to go working on my fundamentals and expanding my stack. The past few years I've worked at Northrop Grumman in the contracts dept, and before that I was playing college basketball at Augusta University. My hobbies include basketball, reading, coding, and tv shows. MY favorite books are star wars, endergs game, and the 33 strategies of war.
